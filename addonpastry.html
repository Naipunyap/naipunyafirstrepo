PASTRY

Pastry cakes are delicious desserts that are made by combining layers of cake with various fillings, such as cream, fruit, or chocolate. They are typically small in size and often served as individual portions. Pastry cakes are known for their exquisite appearance, with beautiful designs and decorations that make them visually appealing. Pastry cakes can come in a variety of flavors and styles, ranging from classic options like chocolate or vanilla to more unique and exotic flavors like matcha, mango, or raspberry. They are often covered in a layer of frosting or icing, and can be further adorned with decorative elements such as fruit slices, chocolate shavings, sprinkles, or edible flowers.
